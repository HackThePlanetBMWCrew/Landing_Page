Ride is...

Whether you're out for a weekend ride with friends or chaisng that endless horizon into the unknown, Ride is your personal sidecar of confidence ensuring that you have the best experience every time you hop on the bike.

Community

Ride was designed with both motorcycle enthusiasts and newcomers in mind in order to enhance the community through making connections and sharing experiences.

Tracking

The active tracking of a variety of metrics, done solely on the mobile device, allow riders to gain insights into and share their and other's rides from around the world.

Planning

Plan your routes based on your own intuition or see where others have ventured. Prepare for a long tour or a short ride both can be achieved with ease.

Wearable

Get all the info you need when you need it. Real time weather updates, riders in your area, live location based updates to social, and more so you can focus on what matters most, the Ride.
